# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple rule: be respectful, constructive, and specific.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss ideas and code, not personal traits.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Assume good intent, but correct problems clearly.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks.
- Spam or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without consent.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation to keep the project productive.
